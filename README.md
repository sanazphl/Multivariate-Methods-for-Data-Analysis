# Multivariate-Methods-for-Data-Analysis
Predictors of Mental Health Score: A Stepwise Multiple Linear Regression Analysis and Factor Analysis Using Community Survey Data.
Previous research shown that substance use is negatively associated with mental health, increasing risks of depression, anxiety and emotional instability (Thompson et al., 2021). Other studies highlight the importance of coping skills, self-esteem and community trust in improving resilience and overall well-being (Smith & Lee, 2022). Based on these findings, the study uses a community dataset (N = 220) to examine which socioeconomic, behavioural and psychosocial factors significantly predict an individual’s overall mental health score.
The rationale for this study is twofold:
1.1 identify the strongest predictors of mental health using stepwise multiple linear regression.
1.2  provide evidence-based recommendations for community programs focused on mental well-being.
